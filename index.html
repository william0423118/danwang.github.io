About Me

I am Director of Machine Learning at Moody's Analytics in New York. My work mainly focuses on natural language processing, deep learning and quantitative methods on RISK (credit, compliance, reputation risk and KYC).
I am currently interested in the areas of machine learning in risk, interpretable machine learning (counterfuctual explanation), and sentiment analysis.
I hold a Ph.D in Financial Engineering, advised by Dr. Florescu. I obtained my Bachelor degree in Beihang University, Beijing.
Besides research and work, my hobbies include basketball, scuba diving, skiing, and travelling.
