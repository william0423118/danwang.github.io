About Me

I am a second year Ph.D student in Financial Engineering, I work with my advisor Dr. Florescu. In 2015, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Beihang University, Beijing.

My research focuses on Machine Learning in Finance and Volatility Spillover. Currently, I am working on a project about Credit Rating and Credit Events prediction using Deep Learning sponsored by UBS.

Research Interest

Machine Learning in Credit Rating

Image encoding: Investiage the 1D to 2D encoding method for financial statement data.

Interpretable Machine Learning: Provide recommendation for public company for better manage company to improve credit rating.

Feature Selection: Dimensionality Reduction, PCA, Genetic Algorithm, Feature Null Importance (permutation).

High Frequency Credit Indicatior: Unbalanced dataset, Z-score, CDS pricing, Merton's model.

NLP: Extract information from 10-K financial report, sentiment analysis, TF-IDF.

Volatility Spillover

BEKK-GARCH model decomposition.

Volatility spillover index construction.

Analysis Volatility spillover on high frequency data in global markets.
